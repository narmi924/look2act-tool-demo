# Limitations

- The current artifact targets Windows desktop only.
- The prototype is not intended to replace infrared eye trackers.
- The prototype does not claim mouse-level precision.
- The main demonstration uses 25-point calibration, which adds setup burden.
- Webcam quality, lighting, glasses, reflections, camera angle, and head pose can affect performance.
- Full dataset, model weights, source code, ONNX files, `.pth` files, and executable binaries are not released in the initial submission.
- Cross-platform packaging is outside the initial artifact boundary.
- The Deep mode remains a research-oriented path.
